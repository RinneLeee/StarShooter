How to Play

  • Objective: Survive as long as possible by managing your four stats: Warmth, Health, Hunger, and Stamina.
  • The Fire: Your 🔥 Warmth drops constantly. The colder it gets, the faster it drops.
  • Feed the Fire: To add warmth, Drag 🪵 Wood from your consumables and drop it onto the 🔥 fire pit.
  • Start a Fire: If the fire goes out, you must restart it. This costs 1 🪵 Wood and 1 🪨 Rock and only has a 40% chance of success.
  • Actions: Use the "Actions" panel to survive. All actions cost Stamina and make you Hungrier.
    • - Gather Wood: Your main way to get 🪵 Wood. An 🪓 Axe makes it *much* more effective.
    • - Go Hunting: Play a minigame to get 🥩 Raw Meat.
    • - Explore: A high-risk action. You might find rare tools (🪓, 🔪) or a 🩹 Medkit at a crash site, or you might get injured.
    • - Craft: Combine materials, like 🪨 Rocks into 🧱 Bricks.
  • Food & Health:
    • - Drag 🥩 Raw Meat to the fire to cook it into 🍖 Cooked Meat.
    • - Eating 🍖 Cooked Meat restores Hunger.
    • - If your Hunger is above 80%, you will slowly regenerate ❤️ Health.
  • Dangers:
    • - If your 🔥 Warmth hits 0, you'll rapidly lose ❤️ Health.
    • - If your 🥩 Hunger hits 0, you'll starve and lose ❤️ Health.
    • - FOOD POISONING: Eating 🥩 Raw Meat has a 50% chance to poison you (💀). You'll lose health over time and your ⚡ Stamina will be capped at 70%.
    • - Animal Attacks: You can be randomly attacked by Wolves or Bears. A 🔪 Knife gives you a chance to automatically scare them off.
