Instructions

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these instructions carefully to prepare and process your data correctly.

  1. 1

    Prepare Data in Excel

    In your spreadsheet, you must convert all empty cells to contain the word `NULL`. The easiest way is to use the 'Find and Replace' feature (Ctrl+H). In the 'Find what:' field, leave it blank. In the 'Replace with:' field, type `NULL`. Then click 'Replace All'.

  2. 2

    Copy to a Text File

    Once the replacements are done, select all cells in your sheet (Ctrl+A) and copy them (Ctrl+C). Open a plain text editor like Notepad, paste the data (Ctrl+V), and save the file with a `.txt` extension.

  3. 3

    Upload and Process

    Return to the Home page. Click the file upload area to select your saved `.txt` file, and then click the 'Process' button to load your data into the tool.

  4. 4

    Filter Your Results

    Use the Super Filter section to analyze your data. You can select a column, search for specific values, and select multiple values at once to apply as filters.

  5. 5

    Download Filtered Data

    After applying your desired filters, click the 'Download Filtered (.csv)' button. This will save a new CSV file to your computer containing only the data that matches your criteria.
